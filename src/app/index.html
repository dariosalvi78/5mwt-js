5-meter walk test

Instructions

The test consists in walking 3 times over a distance of 5 meters.

Select a straight path in your home such as a corridor. Mark two points at a measured distance of 5 meters.

To start the test, press the "start" button. When prompted by this application, walk the first 5 meters, then press the "stop" button. Turn around, press the "start" button again and walk back to the start point. Press the "stop" button and repeat the test one more time.

At the end of the test, the average time will be displayed.
